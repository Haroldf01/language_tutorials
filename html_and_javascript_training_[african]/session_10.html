Assignment Operators

=, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=, **=

Comparison Operators

==, ===, !=, !==, >, >=, <, <=


== | Is Equal to

=== | Identical (equal and of the same type)

!= | Not Equal to

!== | Not Identical

> | Greater Than

>= | Greater Than or equal to

< | Less Than

<= | Less Than or equal to
